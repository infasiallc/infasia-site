Privacy Policy

How we handle the information you share with us.

Last updated: 1 June 2026

Who we are

Infasia LLC ("Infasia", "we", "us") is a limited liability company registered in Wyoming, United States. You can reach us any time at info@infasia.net.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us. When you submit our contact form or email us, we collect the details you provide — typically your name, company, work email, area of interest, and your message.
  • Basic technical data. Like most websites, our hosting and security providers automatically record standard request information (such as IP address, browser type, and pages requested) to operate and protect the site.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and arrange a call.
  • To provide, and follow up on, the services you ask about.
  • To keep the website secure and working properly.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

Service providers

We rely on a small number of trusted providers to run this site and our communications. They process data only to provide their service to us:

  • GitHub Pages — hosts this website.
  • Cloudflare — provides DNS, security, and email routing for our @infasia.net address.
  • FormSubmit — processes contact-form submissions and delivers them to us by email.

Cookies and analytics

This website does not use advertising or tracking cookies, and we do not run third-party analytics that profile you. Any data handling is limited to what is needed to deliver and secure the site.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry messages only as long as needed to respond and to maintain ordinary business records, after which they are deleted.

Your choices and rights

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information you have shared with us. Just email info@infasia.net and we will help.

International processing

We are based in the United States, and information you send us may be processed there.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email info@infasia.net.
