The Trust and Control framework

Security is the differentiator, not a checkbox.

For regulated and data-sensitive operators, control is the reason to buy. Every Infasia deployment is built on a framework designed to keep you in command of your data, your agents, and their actions.

On-premises by default Per-client isolation Air-gapped option
  • Encryption at rest and in transit, with data residency you can attest to.
  • Role-based access with single sign-on and multi-factor authentication.
  • Least privilege per agent, each with its own scoped service identity.
  • Human-in-the-loop approval gates for high-impact actions, plus an emergency stop.
  • An immutable, per-agent audit trail of every prompt, decision, and action.
