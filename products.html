Pillar 04 / AI Products

Subscription AI products, built and run by us.

Infasia builds standalone AI products on our own infrastructure, using our own data and sources, and offers them to subscribers through a simple app. It is the highest-margin, most scalable pillar, and it stands entirely on its own.

The product line is in development. The sections below describe the model and direction.

The model

Ready-made AI you can simply subscribe to

No project, no deployment on your side. You sign up, sign in, and use it. The product runs on Infasia infrastructure, and you reach it from wherever you work.

🏢

We build and host it

Each product is developed and operated entirely on Infasia's own servers, with our own data and sources behind it.

💳

You subscribe

Simple subscription access, priced for the value of the product, with no infrastructure for you to stand up or maintain.

📱

Use it anywhere

Reach the product through an app that suits you: web, desktop, or mobile. Sign in and get to work.

Why this pillar matters

The most scalable thing we do.

A single product can serve many subscribers at once, which makes it the highest-margin and most scalable part of Infasia. It takes real platform and go-to-market investment to do well, and we treat it as a long-term build.

  • Runs on Infasia infrastructure, with our own data and sources.
  • Subscription access through a clean, simple app.
  • One platform serving many subscribers, so it scales.
  • Independent of any services engagement we deliver.

Example direction

A tax-document preparation app

One product we may build is a tax-document preparation agent: a subscriber signs in, provides what is needed, and the product prepares the documents. It runs on our infrastructure and data, and it is offered straight to subscribers as software. It is an illustration of the direction, not a launched product. illustrative

  • Sign up and use it the same day.
  • No deployment, servers, or setup on the subscriber's side.
  • Available on the device the subscriber prefers.
